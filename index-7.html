Barcelona best of with a Van - First time Private tour

Barcelona is an enchanting seaside city with boundless culture, fabled architecture and a world-class drinking and dining scene, there's no other place like it!
One of the oldest cities in the world, Barcelona is widely considered Europe's coolest city, full of interesting, artistic and fashionable people full of history, some of the world's most famous architecture, beaches, the superb Mediterranean food, and a very cosmopolitan feel make Barcelona the place to be, and it's no surprise that the city has taken over from cities like Paris and London as a more favored short break in a city.
With a new fully air conditioned 8 seat Van you will be able to drive through Barcelona looking at the most beautiful sites, with a buildings, monuments and neighborhoods with the explanations of an experienced guide with a lot of photography stops and information of a local, what to do or where to buy your favorite things, or where to enjoy a good meal and more
