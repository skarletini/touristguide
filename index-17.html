Sephardic Jewish treasures of Girona and Besalu

Itenerary

After Hotel pick up we will be driving about an hour to Girona.
We are going to walk through the fortified city and the Jewish quarter. We will enter the Jewish Museum.
Then, we will continue to Besalu .
Optional we could have a lunch there at one of the most special restaurants in the area. No kosher
Than we will discover the beauty of this medieval city and walk throughout the Jewish neighborhood.
Finally we will drive back to Barcelona
