Pyrenees the Valley of Nuria, the stonebuild Queralbs village and shopping

A whole world of experiences from the start. Vall de Núria summer and ski resort, a breath away from the french borders, with a unique wealth of nature and landscape, is located in the Eastern Pyrenees, within the municipal area of Queralbs. It sits above one of the highest points of the Ribes Valley and it's surrounded by peaks of almost 9, 000 feet from which flow a multitude of water sources and torrents. I invite you to glory in this spectacular mountain landscape using a unique and unusual form of transport: the rack railway. It covers a distance of 12. 5 km and takes you up an incline of over 3000 feet. The rack railway is situated next to the last Spanish village of Queralbs, a unique stone build millenary village, that we are going to visit too. After our incredible experience of tranquility and nature , we will be heading to the very famous outlet village "La Roca Village" where we are going to discover a world of luxury , the region's ultimate shopping destination, just 40 minutes from Barcelona

Itenerary

Pick up from your Hotel. After about an hour and a half driving time we will reach Queralbs , where we are going to have a lovely tour through the stone build village on the cliffs of the Pyrenees. We will take the rack railway up to the Valley of Nuria. There will be plenty of time for hiking , having a lunch or enjoying the peaks of Puigmal on between 8 and 9000 feet altitude with the cable car !!
After that we will drive to La Roca outlet shopping Village witch is located almost on our way back to Barcelona . There will be also plenty of time for shopping , before we drive back to your Hotel in Barcelona
