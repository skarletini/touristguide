Hello, I am Lydia from Barcelona an experienced guide and driver.
I give my private tours of highest quality standards {you are not part of a bigger group} by sharing my knowledge in an engaging, illuminating and entertaining way, customizing my tours to the needs of each and every visitor, making your tours with me, one of your most unforgettable experiences.
I am friendly, flexible, open to serendipity, passionate about my job that can be contagious and inspiring, and make the site really come alive.
As a local guide, of course you won't get only the touristic sites but also the hidden treasures of my city !
My goal is to make you enjoy each and every moment with me in my very beautiful city and its fantastic surroundings !
I own 2 private 8 and 6 seat fully air conditioned Vans that make transportation around Barcelona city as well to other Catalan cities and sites easy and pleasant and without any extra transportation cost !

Nikol

BARCELONA, CATALUNYA, Spain

Hello, My name is Nicole and I work as a licensed tourist guide in Barcelona.
I started my work in the tourism first as a Tour leader traveling around all Europe but after 10 years of wandering I decided that it was time to settle in, in my most favorite city in Europe and that is Barcelona!!!
My passion and also my studies is History and my hobby is Art and I am getting fascinated every time when I am walking along medieval castles or ancient sites!! Even my guests see this and they are keep saying to me that I bring history to life!!!

Now I have a long experience as a guide too and whether you focus in history, Art or just to get to know my city and have fun I am your perfect match
