Barcelona Full Panorama and Sagrada Familia with a Van

Barcelona is an enchanting seaside city with boundless culture, fabled architecture and a world-class drinking and dining scene, like climbing into a surrealist movie, there's no other place like it! One of the oldest cities in the world, filled with architectural masterpieces that you don't often come across in Europe. Taking a stroll through the shadowy lanes of the Gothic quarter, its a window into Roman-era and to the Middle Ages, while in other parts of town bloom the sculptural masterpieces of Modernism, by Gaudí and his Catalan architectural contemporaries , for which this city is so well known. Barcelona is the home of one of the World's most famous streets La Rambla . the most complete food market in the world La Boqueria and the home to the third best beach in the world. The Magical Montjuïic Hill, is the best place to start, offering first-rate museums with panoramic views with the deep blue Mediterranean sea beckon and the and the sun-drenched beaches making a fine backdrop.
