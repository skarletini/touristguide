Tarragona-Sitges-Roc de Sant Gaietà

Itenerary

We take the highway to Tarragona where we are going to walk the old medieval walled quarter and the Roman ruins, as the circus and the Amphitheater. The "Balcony of Mediterrani" , the Ramblas. Optional we can have a typical Catalan lunch. Than we will take the coastal road to Roc de Sant Gaietà, we will scroll around to admire the picturesque views and architecture and than we will proceed to Sitges ! We will enjoy a walk around the lovely old part of this coastal gem and optional we can enjoy a cup of coffee or a cooling drink on the beach side !
