Costa Brava the bluest sea, sites and taste - private tour

Why should I go to Costa Brava? Because it's one of the most romantic, gorgeous, unspoiled stretches of coast in Europe. Where else, can you drive or walk alongside rugged pink rocks with the teal-colored Mediterranean glimmering below, framed by grand, arabesque pines? Gloriously wild in parts and tastefully manicured in others, the Costa has some of the finest sandy stretches to elegant horseshoe bays and secluded smugglers' coves. We will head for the stretch of coast between Pals and Palamos. Pals , the walled hilltop medieval town , looks like a spectacular Hollywood scenery and the genteel horseshoe bays of Llafranc and Calella de Palafrugell are two of the prettiest on the world. In Calella de Palafrugell at the wildest corner of Costa Brava we will spend some free time to taste the exceptional see food or any other meal you like and optional swim on the bluest part of the Mediterranean sea !
