Barcelona with kids private tour

If you are travelling with kids a city tour can be boring for them and tiring.
Why not choose something that includes some fun for them ?
Besides some best of Barcelona like Sagrada Familia, like the panoramic view of our City from Montjuic Hill, we will visit the spectacular Aquarium of Barcelona of 11.000 species of fishes from all over the world, the very tasty Museum of Chocolate with some very tempting chocolate sculptures and finally we can choose between the Tibidabo amusement park of the Barcelona lovely zoo
