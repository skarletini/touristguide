Tarragona-Sitges-Roc de Sant Gaietà

A city full of surprises with thousands of years of civilization just waiting to be discovered. Walk next to the Roman circus, or visit the Roman Amphitheatre Building that held fights between gladiators or lose yourself in the charming, narrow alleys of the historic quarter, where the essence of the medieval city remains untouched by time, or enjoy a lunch surrounded by over 2, 000 years of history. This is the true World Heritage of Tarragona! It is a heritage made up of monuments that transcend time, of spots that take us back to Roman, medieval, modern and modernist eras; Tarragona, the essence of the Mediterranean! Along the fine golden sand beach , called Costa Daurada visit a Rock that houses some incredible spanish architecture buildings and finally enjoy a coffee or drink in the famous coastal town of Sitges on our way back to Barcelona!
