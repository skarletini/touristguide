3 days Exploring Barcelona and Catalonia!

Barcelona is a wonderful city, but there is much more to discover in Catalonia too.
After a full panorama city tour with a Sagrada Familia church visit it's time to explore the main sites of our state !
Catalonia has a rich history, beautiful and varied landscapes and amazing local food and wine.
Getting out of its capital to explore the area is an absolute must !
There are a number of gorgeous, medieval villages and cities that are worth the drive out of the city.
Most of these can be only reached by car, as they are planted in the middle of the beautiful Catalan countryside.
Journey back in time as you discover these charming medieval and ancient places and the stunning countryside outside Barcelona
